### Features
- Upload video (30 secs to max 1 min long)
- View a feed of video
- Follow users
- Performing actions on videos (comments, likes)

### Non-Functional Requirements
- Highly available (not strong consistent)
- Latency

### Stats
- 1ML DAU
- 5MB per video
